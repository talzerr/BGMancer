# What's Coming

## Player

- **Seekbar** — click or drag to jump anywhere in the current track
- **Track rating** — Thumbs Up / Thumbs Down while a track plays: thumbs up marks it as a keeper, thumbs down removes it and nudges the AI away from that vibe for that game next time
- **Chapter markers for Full OST videos** — when a long compilation is playing, show timestamp notches on the seekbar; hover to see the chapter name, click to jump there
- **Repeat modes** — off / repeat all / repeat one
- **Mini video view** — expand the player to show the actual YouTube video

## Playlist

- **Per-track reroll** — swap out a single track you don't like without regenerating the whole playlist
- **Weighted distribution** — give a favourite game more track slots than others
- **Vibe balancing** — automatically alternate between high-energy and chill tracks

## Library

- **Playlist cache refresh** — a button per game to force BGMancer to re-discover its YouTube playlist (useful when an OST upload gets taken down or replaced)
- **Bulk text import** — paste a list of game titles to add multiple games at once

## Seeds (Shareable Configs)

A **seed** is a short string that encodes your entire game library — titles, vibes, and Full OST flags. Share it with a friend and they can recreate your exact setup and hit Generate.

- **Export seed** — copy your current library as a seed string
- **Import seed** — paste a seed to load someone else's setup
- **Share link** — a URL that auto-imports a seed when opened, no sign-in needed
- **Curated presets** — built-in seeds for common setups like "Soulsborne Boss Rush", "JRPG Ambience", or "Cosy Exploration" as one-click starting points

## YouTube

- **Sync to an existing playlist** — push to a YouTube playlist you already own instead of creating a new one each time
- **Multiple playlists** — maintain separate playlists for different moods

## Quality of Life

- **Drag to reorder** — manually rearrange the track list before syncing to YouTube
- **Mobile install** — add BGMancer to your home screen as a PWA
